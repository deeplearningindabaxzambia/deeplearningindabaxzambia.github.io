The Goal of Deep Learning Indaba X

The goal of the Deep Learning Indaba X is to promote the usage and involvement of young people in Machine Learning with the purpose of producing a cohort of youths who are technologically empowered and skilled to generate a livelihood that makes them self-reliant and able to contribute towards National Development

Objectives

In order to realize the project goal, the project will pursue the following objectives:

  • Promote innovative applied research in Machine Learning and foster the links between government’s services, industry, academia and market;
  • Establish a framework for researchers from different fields to achieve a common objective;
  • Increase the understanding and appreciation of Machine Learning among students by providing a learning environment for students to gain a “Machine Learning” experience;
  • To provide guidance for research (current and future) concerning the importance and potential of current and future Machine Learning developments.
